Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bible Plus free to use?

Yes! Bible Plus is free to download and use. The free tier includes the personalized feed, Bible reader, basic AI companion (3 messages per week), 1 audio Bible chapter per day, 4 soundscapes, and 1 saved collection. Bible+ Pro unlocks unlimited everything.

What does Bible+ Pro include?

Bible+ Pro gives you unlimited AI companion messages, unlimited audio Bible chapters, all 9 narrator voices, all 11 soundscapes, all 52 backgrounds, and unlimited saved collections. Plans start at $4.99/week or $49.99/year with a 3-day free trial.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel anytime through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > Bible Plus > Cancel Subscription. You'll still have access until the end of your billing period. You can also manage subscriptions at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your profile data, saved verses, and collections are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We don't have user accounts or databases that store your personal information. AI chat messages are processed transiently and not retained. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.

How does the AI companion work?

The AI companion is powered by advanced language models and trained to provide warm, pastoral guidance rooted in Scripture. It always includes real Bible verses in your preferred translation. It's designed for spiritual encouragement, not as a replacement for pastoral care or professional counseling.

What Bible translations are available?

Bible Plus currently supports KJV (King James Version) and NIV (New International Version) translations. You can set your preferred translation in Settings and the AI companion will use it when referencing Scripture.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open Bible Plus, go to Settings, scroll down to the About section, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used to purchase the subscription.

Can I use Bible Plus offline?

Most features work offline, including the Bible reader, your saved verses, and the personalized feed. The AI companion and audio Bible require an internet connection since they use cloud-based services.
